### Colors

The main brand colors are defined in the CSS. You can easily customize them:


### Images

Replace `family-image.png` with your own image. Recommended dimensions:

- **Format:** PNG, JPG, or WebP
- **Dimensions:** 600x800px (or similar aspect ratio)
- **File size:** Under 500KB for optimal loading

### Text Content

Edit the content directly in `index.html`:

- Hero title and tagline
- Description text
- Button labels
- Footer email
